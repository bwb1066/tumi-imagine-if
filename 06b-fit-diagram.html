Porter · How it fits together

The cubes were designed for this carry-on.

It's not coincidence. Tumi builds the 19 Degree's interior compression frame to fit the cubes Tumi makes — the medium fills the main compartment, the small fits shoes, and the third fits toiletries against the divider. Other cubes work; these are the dimensional match.

Medium cube · 14" × 10" × 4"

Fills the main compartment to within 0.4". Compresses to 60% volume, lets you pack 8 days of business in the carry-on. Mesh top so you can see the contents at security.

Small cube · 9" × 6" × 4"

Sized to two pairs of dress shoes plus a sneaker, or three rolled t-shirts. Goes against the wheel-housing wall — protects shoes from the bag's shape memory.

Third cube · 11" × 5" × 3"

Sits against the lid divider. TSA-friendly window for liquids. The handle of the carry-on extends through it without compression.
